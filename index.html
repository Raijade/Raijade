Meditation

Have you ever wondered what things you can do to help releive stress? Here I will discuss 5 ways to bring about peace, relaxtion and strength all which can be done from the comfort of your home or wherever there is space.

There are different ways you can meditate. Below I will touch on a few that I find helpful either everyday or some are done whenever I need to take a moment for myself.

Benefits from meditation include

  • Gaining a new perspective on stressful situations
  • Increasing self-awareness
  • Improving sleep quality
  • Reducing negative emotions
  • Focusing on the present

Mantra Meditation

Mantra meditation is done in a form of sitting down, legs crossed and chanting a single word or phrase while bringing focus to self. You can do this in any duration you would like. Some do it for 5 mins, others will repeat a mantra up to 30mins. Whatever works best for you will give you the most benefit.

Here are 7 easy steps to get you started

  1. Get comfortable.
  2. Set a timer.
  3. Start with a few deep breaths.
  4. Use your mantra.
  5. Let your breath guide you.
  6. Remember to gently redirect wandering thoughts.
  7. Close the meditation.
