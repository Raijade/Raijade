Self

Mindfullness meditation is a sense of self. Being aware of who you are and what is in your surroundings. You take a deep dive into the unkown while exploring your mind body and soul. Letting go of your mental, emotional and physical thoughts that are taking up space in your brain.

Meditation is by far the most effective way to clear you mind. There is so much clutter that goes into ones mind that it is hard to filter out. It only takes a few mins at any given time to explore you inner self and let light in.

Nature

Sometimes people find meditation boring. They don't see the purporse in it. But as with anything you have to make the most of it. Being outside in nature can perhaps center you and make you be one with the earth...grounded as they say. Nature has a funny way of showing us what can be if you just take the time out to listen.
